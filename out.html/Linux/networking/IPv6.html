Disabling IPv6

In Ubuntu, IPv6 can be disabled in /etc/sysctl.conf 
net.ipv6.conf.all.disable_ipv6 = 1
net.ipv6.conf.default.disable_ipv6 = 1
net.ipv6.conf.lo.disable_ipv6 = 1
Apparently, Hadoop needs IPv6 to be IPv6
