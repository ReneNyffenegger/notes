mp3splt

Split foo.mp3 into 10 equal sized chunks: 
mp3splt -S 10 foo.mp3

Output format

mp3splt -S 10 -o @n foo.mp3
Creates 01.mp3 .. 10.mp3.

Frame mode

Sometimes I had to use frame mode ( -f).
