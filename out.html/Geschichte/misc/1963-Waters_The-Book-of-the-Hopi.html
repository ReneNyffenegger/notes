Erinnerung der Hopi an die Flut

In diesem Buch werden die Erinnerungen der Hopi an die Flut geschildert:
After a gigantic flood, their ancestors used many family-sized rafts and “island hopped” for many years north and east to the Americas. The steep coast line forced them along the coast until they could land. Rising water later drowned the chain of islands.
